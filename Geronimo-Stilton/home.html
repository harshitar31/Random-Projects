Kingdom of Fantasy

Geronimo Stilton

Geronimo Stilton is a brave and curious journalist who finds himself embarking on thrilling adventures across the Kingdom of Fantasy, despite his initial reluctance. With his sharp mind and big heart, he uncovers mysteries and faces challenges while learning valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and believing in oneself.

Blossom

Blossom, the queen of Kingdom of Fantasy, is a wise and magical fairy who helps guide Geronimo through the Kingdom of Fantasy with her knowledge and gentle nature. She is always ready to lend a hand with her magical powers, offering support and advice in their epic adventures.

Cackle

Cackle is a mischievous and tricky sorceress who often creates chaos in the Kingdom of Fantasy with her dark magic. She is known for her cunning nature and frequently tries to thwart Geronimo and his friends, making her a constant source of trouble.

Scribble Hopper

Scribblehopper is a talking frog that writes stories and poems. He appears in 11 books about the Kingdom of Fantasy. His book, the legendarium, is an informational guide to the kingdom of fantasy.

Mel

Mel was commonly referred as the Great Wizard of Light by many creatures of the Kingdom of Fantasy because he was very wise and would come with open arms to any creature of the Kingdom that needed his guidance.

Lady of Dreams

The Lady of Dreams lives and rules the Mountain of Sweet Dreams, which is in the Kingdom of Fairies. She is a member of the Secret Alliance.

Clever

Clever Chameleon is a member of the Lizard Population and is the wisest of his Tribe. Besides being wise and pure in mind, he knows martial arts but only uses such skill to defend those in desperate need.

Menace

Menace, often nicknamed "The Chattering Cat" is one of Wither's assistants. Many of the creatures of the Kingdom of Fantasy say Menace is disgusting, rude, and dishonest. He is often seen in the Witch School of Magic in the Kingdom of Witches.

Tick Tock

He is the current ruler of the Land of Time, and he was the former ruler of The Kingdom of Fantasy. He is also the father of Prince Lucky, Queen Blossom, Princess Sproutness, Queen Wither, and the adoptive father of Geronimo, before the end of The Guardian of the Realm.
